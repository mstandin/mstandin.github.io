Introduction

Icing on the Cake

Icing on the Cake is a family-owned pastry shop that specializes in sweet baked goods and cakes. Customers can stop by the storefront to purchase baked goods that are made fresh every day.

Target audience

The people who use this site are people interested in local baked goods and pastries. This can include people new to the area and looking for more information on nearby businesses, people who want to support local shops, and regular customers who want to try something new.

Their primary tasks on the site would be checking what items are on the menu and finding more information about the shop like location and contact info. Their goals are to learn more about the shop and find food they want to try.

About

Icing on the Cake first opened its doors in 2001 in sunny San Luis Obispo right after founder and head chef Kara Ermelinda graduated from Cal Poly. Since then, Kara has developed a unique roster of pasteries and cakes using fresh, local ingredients. All pasteries are baked fresh each morning to ensure that they are high quality. Icing on the Cake's goal is to provide San Luis Obispo with delicious baked goods to enjoy. We hope to see you in the shop soon!
